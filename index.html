About Me

Hello! My name is Brandon Ruggles, and I'm a senior computer science major at NJIT. When I was younger, I was always amazed by computers and the fact that they could generate entire worlds in the blink of an eye. Over time, that amazement grew into curiousity, and I decided to delve into the world of programming. During my freshman year of high school, I took my first programming class (a simple Visual Basic and C# course), and I ended up liking it so much that I decided programming was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. Today, I've worked on multiple programming projects on my own, as well as with others, in a variety of languages and on a variety of different platforms. Being curious by nature, I've involved myself in various aspects of programming including app development, web development, scripting on Linux/Unix, and game development. As of late, I've taken an increased interest in lower-level graphics programming and artificial intelligence. My dream is to one day use my programming abilities to change the world for the better, whether it be through a creation of my own, or through my contribution to a project created at a software company.
